sort1 uses: Bubble Sort

How do you know?: Bubble Sort uses the lowest lower bound running time (among the three) of omega(n).

sort2 uses: Merge Sort

How do you know?: Merge Sort uses almost the same running time for all the three conditions (random, sorted and reversed).

sort3 uses: Selection Sort

How do you know?: Selection Sort uses the highest lower bound running time (among the three) of omega(n^2).
